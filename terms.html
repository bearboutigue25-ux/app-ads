Subscription

The app uses an auto-renewable subscription. Payment will be charged to your Apple ID after the free trial period ends or after confirmation of purchase. The subscription automatically renews unless it is canceled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period.

Subscription Cancellation

Users can manage or cancel their subscription at any time in their Apple ID settings:

Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions

After cancellation, the subscription will remain active until the end of the current billing period.
