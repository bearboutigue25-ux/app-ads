MagicBrushBearBoutigue

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 18.01.2026

This Privacy Policy describes how MagicBrushBearBoutigue and Notes by Bear Boutique and Music Bears (“the App”, “we”, “us”, or “our”) collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our application.

We are committed to protecting your privacy.

By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Do NOT Collect

Your privacy is important to us.

The App does NOT collect, store, or transmit any of the following data:

  • Personal information (name, email address, postal address, phone number)
  • Financial information
  • Location data
  • Contact information
  • Browsing history
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Analytics data
  • Any data stored on your device outside of the App

We do not create user accounts and do not require registration.

2. Drawings and Media Files

The App allows users to create drawings and:

  • save them to the device’s photo library (with user permission)
  • save them to an internal gallery within the App
  • view a built-in gallery of bear illustrations

All drawings remain only on the user’s device.

We do not send, upload, or store drawings on any external servers.

3. Access to Device Permissions

The App may request access to the following permissions:

  • Photos — Used only to save drawings created in the App.
  • Camera — Used only when you manually choose to take a photo.
  • Microphone — Used only locally if voice features exist.

The App will never access these permissions without your explicit consent.

4. Children’s Privacy

The App is suitable for children and does not collect personal data. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

5. Third-Party Services

The App does not use:

  • analytics tools
  • tracking services
  • advertising networks
  • external data storage

No user data is shared with third parties.

6. Data Security

Since the App does not collect or store personal information, no data is transmitted to external servers. All your content remains securely on your device.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in the App. Updates will be posted here with a new “Last updated” date.
