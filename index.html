Privacy Policy

Last updated: 18.01.2026

This Privacy Policy describes how MagicBrushBearBoutigue and Notes by Bear Boutique and Music Bears (“the App”, “we”, “us”, or “our”) collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our application.

We are committed to protecting your privacy.

By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

⸻

1. Information We Do NOT Collect

Your privacy is important to us.

The App does NOT collect, store, or transmit any of the following data:

  • Personal information (name, email address, postal address, phone number)
  • Financial information
  • Location data
  • Contact information
  • Browsing history
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Analytics data
  • Any data stored on your device outside of the App

We do not create user accounts and do not require registration.

⸻

2. Drawings and Media Files

The App allows users to create drawings and:

  • save them to the device’s photo library (with user permission)
  • save them to an internal gallery within the App
  • view a built-in gallery of bear illustrations

All drawings remain only on the user’s device.

We do not send, upload, or store drawings on any external servers.

⸻

3. Access to Device Permissions

The App may request access to the following permissions:

  • Photos
    Used only to save drawings created in the App.
    We do not access your existing photos — only the image you choose to save.
  • Camera
    Used only when you manually choose to take a photo for use within the App.
    No camera data is collected or stored by us.
  • Microphone (optional)
    If the App supports voice features, microphone access is used only locally and is not stored or transmitted.
    The App will never access these permissions without your explicit consent.
⸻

4. Children’s Privacy

The App is suitable for children and does not collect personal data.

We comply with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

⸻

5. Third-Party Services

The App does not use:

  • analytics tools
  • tracking services
  • advertising networks
  • external data storage

No user data is shared with third parties.

⸻

6. Data Security

Since the App does not collect or store personal information, no data is transmitted to external servers.

All your content remains securely on your device.

⸻

7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Notes by Bear Boutique (“the App”, “we”, “us”, or “our”) handles user information.

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it.

By using the Notes by Bear Boutique app, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

⸻

1. Information We Do NOT Collect

Your privacy is important to us.

The Notes by Bear Boutique app does NOT collect, store, or transmit the following data:

  • Personal information (name, email address, phone number, postal address)
  • Financial information
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Browsing history
  • Tracking or analytics data

We do not require account registration.

⸻

2. Notes, Drawings, and User Content

The App allows users to:

  • create and edit notes
  • draw and create illustrations
  • save drawings only to the device’s photo gallery
  • view a built-in gallery of bear illustrations
  • store notes using cloud synchronization (iCloud)

All notes, drawings, and content belong to the user.

We do not upload or store user content on external servers outside of Apple’s iCloud services.

⸻

3. Device Permissions

The App may request access to the following device permissions only with the user’s explicit consent:

  • Microphone — for voice notes or audio features
  • Camera — when the user chooses to take photos for notes
  • Photo Gallery — to save drawings created in the App

The App never accesses device permissions without your explicit consent.

⸻

4. Cloud Storage (iCloud)

The App may use Apple iCloud to synchronize notes across the user’s devices.

All cloud data is handled exclusively by Apple under its privacy and security standards.

We do not have access to users’ iCloud data.

⸻

5. Children’s Privacy

The Notes by Bear Boutique app is suitable for children and does not knowingly collect personal data.

We comply with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

⸻

6. Third-Party Services

The App does not use:

  • advertising networks
  • third-party analytics
  • tracking services

The App relies only on Apple system services (such as iCloud).

⸻

7. Data Security

User content is stored locally on the device or securely via Apple iCloud.

No personal data is transmitted to external servers controlled by us.
